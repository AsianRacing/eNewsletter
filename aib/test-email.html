International Horse Sports Confederation Elects Winfried Engelbrecht-Bresges as New Vice-President

The International Horse Sports Confederation (IHSC) announced that the IHSC General Assembly unanimously elected Mr Winfried Engelbrecht-Bresges, Chairman of the Asian Racing Federation (ARF), as the Vice-President of the IHSC. He previously served as the standing representative of the International Federation of Horseracing Authority (IFHA) on the IHSC General Assembly.


Mr Engelbrecht-Bresges will become the third Vice-President for the IHSC, succeeding Louis Romanet, who is the Chairman Emeritus of the IFHA.

The International Horse Sports Confederation (IHSC) is a not-for profit, created by the Fédération Equestre Internationale (FEI) and the IFHA as the first formal vehicle for co-operation between the world’s leading governing bodies for equestrian sport.
